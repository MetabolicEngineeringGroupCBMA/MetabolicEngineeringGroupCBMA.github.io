Super Optimal Broth. For 1 L medium, mix the following components in a Schott bottle:
- 20 g / L Tryptone or Peptone
- 5 g / L Yeast extrac
- 0.5 g / L NaCl
- 1 mL KCl 2.5 M


Autoclave, cool to room temperature and add:
- 5 ml of a sterile 2M [[MgCl2]]
- 5 mL of sterile 2M [[MgSO4]]

Alternatively, the Tryptone and Yeast Extract can be autoclaved and the rest of the components can be added from sterile stock solutions:
- 1 mL / L 250 g/L [[NaCl]]
- 1 mL / L 2.5 M [[KCl]]
- 5 ml  /L of a sterile 2M [[MgCl2]]
- 5 mL / L of sterile 2M [[MgSO4]]
