TAE buffer is commonly prepared as a 50× stock solution for laboratory use.

This stock solution can be diluted 49:1 with water to make a 1× working solution.

| Name                                                                      | Per 1 mole | 50x solution | 50×        | 1× solution |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------- | ------------ | ---------- | ----------- |
| [Tris base](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tris_base "Tris base")          | 121.1 g/L  | 2 M          | 242.2 g/L  | 40 mM       |
| [acetic acid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acetic_acid "Acetic acid")    | 57.1 ml/L  | 1 M          | 57.1 ml/L  | 20 mM       |
| [EDTA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/EDTA "EDTA") disodium salt dihydrate | 372.24 g/L | 50 mM        | 18.612 g/L | 1 mM        |
**IMPORTANT!** Use Tris base, **not** Tris-HCl.
