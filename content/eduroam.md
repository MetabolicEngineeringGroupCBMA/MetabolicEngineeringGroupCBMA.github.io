To connect to [eduroam at the Universidade do Minho](http://www.scom.uminho.pt/Default.aspx?tabid=8&pageid=368&lang=pt-PT), you need to select the "eduroam" network, then enter your full University of Minho email address (e.g., `a12345@alunos.uminho.pt` for students or `username@scom.uminho.pt` for employees) as your username and your email account password as the password. The authentication method is [EAP-TTLS with PAP](http://www.scom.uminho.pt/print.aspx?tabid=7&pageid=279&lang=pt-PT), with the data encryption being [TKIP](https://www.google.com/search?num=10&newwindow=1&sca_esv=70a532a702b7bdb6&cs=0&sxsrf=AE3TifNhMcqSEU0IBmDsKesImqlz4z4qWw%3A1758879187738&q=TKIP&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjsybXJj_aPAxX-TqQEHc0SIncQxccNegQIBBAC&mstk=AUtExfB4oPI4_G_ipAolXNlEFHMux3KyyjmhPdzWe4WJmVB2O0gNAqHt_UDR3aS_6cqLqBrl8KzMNE_l9sSz0t2HwsMxjXdgtiWZAnMLu6jo_Qp-OU3RAD9srSeMtd3TQ2d3q_K3veVKiMHTdxvwhvGRqvMQqlgpec_rq1cAFnQWr5mUxaOdEQTWsO098oWarc_aWZ96l8WBjcyySdv91DDOkzioo1FLOiPLziFkAqw661qL2eUgZfot3H4ZNhGVn-FdJKf-X1m3gmrmLb-oPifKdHs_0QGO6V8aOMvqPsobMw2iZQ&csui=3). 

Connection Steps:

1. **Select the "eduroam" network**: from the list of available Wi-Fi networks. 
2. **Enter your username**: in the format: `your_id@your_email_domain.uminho.pt`. 
3. **Enter your password**: which is the password for your Universidade do Minho email account. 
4. Click Connect or Join. 

Important Details: 

- **Network:** eduroam (all lowercase)
- **Authentication Method:** IEEE 802.1x / WPA
- **EAP Type:** EAP-TTLS
- **Inner Authentication:** PAP
- **Data Encryption:** TKIP
