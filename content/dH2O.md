### dH2O ( deionized water )

This is second purest water available in the Department.
This water is also called `ultra-pure` or `agua-pura` in some protocols.

We use this water for:

- Agarose gel electrophoresis, buffers, gels etc.
- Microbial growth media

For some applications double deionized water [[ddH2O]] may be necessary.
