This solution can be used as stock for both [[LB]] and [[YPD]] medium.

- This solution is 4x concentrated for [[LB]] and [[files/wiki/YPD#YPDlight or 1/2-strength YPD or 0.5xYPD\|YPDlight]].
- This solution is 2x concentrated for [[YPD]].

### Mix
- 40 g/L peptone or tryptone
- 20 g/L yeast extract

Autoclave and store at room temp.
