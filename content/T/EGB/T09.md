# Summary: T9 - Gene Cloning and DNA Analysis in Agriculture

## What is this TP about?
This TP focuses on the application of gene cloning technologies in agriculture.

## Applications
- Transgenic crops
- Pest and disease resistance
- Stress tolerance
- Nutritional improvement

## Broader Issues
- Biosafety
- Ethics
- Regulation
- Environmental impact

## Key Take-Home Message
Agricultural biotechnology combines molecular science with societal responsibility.
