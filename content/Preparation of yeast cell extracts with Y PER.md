# Prepare yeast cell extract with Y-PER

Y-PER is a detergent cell extracts from S. cerevisiae. It can be an advantage for cell extracts preparation
from yeast compared to glass beads as less foam is produced.

he manufacturer has a page about the product [here](https://www.thermofisher.com/order/catalog/product/78990?SID=srch-srp-78990).

Cost of the reagent is about 25 cents per extraction, however the Y-PER reagent is most likely 10 mg/mL SB3-14 (myristyl sulfobetaine).

See this [page](http://www.openwetware.org/wiki/Drummond:Lysis) for more information and this [patent](https://patents.google.com/patent/US7282475B2/en).

The detergent is sold [commercially](https://www.sigmaaldrich.com/catalog/product/sigma/t7763).

Sigma charges about 100 Euro for 5g of SB3-14 (T7763-5G) which can make one liter homemade Y-PER, cutting costs to about half.

Se this [list](http://bitesizebio.com/13507/lazy-cell-lysis) at BitesizeBio for alternative commercial products.
