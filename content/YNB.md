# Protocol for making x 10 times concentrated YNB (Yeast Nitrogen Base) solution

The final concentration of YNB in media depends on the type of YNB

| Component                              | C (g/L) | Ammonium sulfate | Amino acids |
| -------------------------------------- | ------- | ---------------- | ----------- |
| YNB w/o amino acids w ammonium sulfate | 6.7     | ✅                | ✅           |
| YNB                                    | 6.7     | ✅                | ❌           |
| YNB w/o ammonium sulfate               | 1.7     | ❌                | ❌           |

YNB has:

- L-Histidine HCl
- DL-Methionine
- DL-Tryptophan

YNB w ammonium sulfate has 5 g/L ammonium sulfate.


### 10 x YNB w ammonium sulfate w amino acids

Dissolve 13.4 g YNB w (NH4)2SO4 in 200 mL water. Sterilize by autoclaving.


### 10 x YNB w/o ammonium sulfate

Dissolve 13.4 g YNB w/o (NH4)2SO4 in 200 mL water. Sterilize by autoclaving.
