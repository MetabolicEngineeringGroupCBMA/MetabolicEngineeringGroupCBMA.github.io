## GenoSmart (VWR)

Transilluminator with 768 x 582 pixel camera.

Using a GenoSmart you can produce images of electrophoresis gels stained
with any of these dyes:


• Coomassie Blue
• Silver Stain
• SYBR® Gold • SYBR® Green • SYBR® Safe • GelStar®
• Sypro® Red • Sypro® Ruby • Sypro® Orange
• Fluorescein • Rhodamine Red™ • Texas Red®
• Pro-Q® Diamond • Deep Purple™
• Ethidium Bromide

The GenoSmart is also suitable for viewing and capturing
images from any of the following:

• Agar plates of dark, light or two color colonies
• Cells in flasks
• Autoradiographs
• DNA, RNA or protein on membranes
• Spot and slot blots of DNA, RNA or protein
• Cells or solutions in microtitre plates
• DNA or protein macroarrays
